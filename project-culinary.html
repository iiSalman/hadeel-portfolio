CULINARY ARTS INSTITUTE
Commercial Project
EN

The Culinary Arts Institute combines education with hands-on experience through spaces such as classrooms, kitchens, a cookery shop, restaurant, café, and outdoor farm. Inspired by the journey of food preparation, the design guides visitors from farm to kitchen to table, creating an immersive and educational culinary experience.
