AWJAJ HOTEL
Hospitality Project
EN

Awjaj Hotel is a seaside destination inspired by the fluid movements of diving. Located near Ras Sheikh Humaid and the Umluj shipwreck, the design reflects flow, balance, and comfort, offering an immersive experience for sea and exploration enthusiasts.

AR
